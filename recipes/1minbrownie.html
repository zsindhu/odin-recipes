A Brownie in a Mug

Description:

Dessert for one is coming right up with this Single Serve Brownie in a Mug recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 2 tbsp canola oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Steps:

  1. Mix together all ingredients in a ramekin or other oven or microwave safe dish.
  2. Microwave for 1 minute.
